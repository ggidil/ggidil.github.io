About Me

I am currently studying for a MSc degree in Political Sociology at the London School of Economics and Political Science. I have a BA in Political Science and Public Administration and a certificate in Philosophy from Bilkent University. My research interests include digital culture, postcolonial theory and political participation.

I enjoy taking photographs, painting, writing and hiking. I speak Turkish and English fluently.
